War and Peace


Author: Leo Tolstoy


Publication Date: 1869


Genre: Novel, Historical Fiction


Description: "War and Peace" is a novel by the Russian author Leo Tolstoy. It is regarded as one of the central works of world literature and is considered Tolstoy's finest literary achievement. The novel chronicles the French invasion of Russia and the impact of the Napoleonic era on Tsarist society through the stories of five Russian aristocratic families. It explores themes of love, war, politics, and the nature of power.
