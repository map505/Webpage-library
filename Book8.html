Moby-Dick


Author: Herman Melville


Publication Date: 1851


Genre: Novel, Adventure Fiction


Description: "Moby-Dick" is a novel by Herman Melville. It tells the story of the obsessive quest of Ahab, captain of the whaling ship Pequod, for revenge on Moby Dick, a giant white sperm whale that had previously bitten off Ahab's leg at the knee during an earlier voyage. The novel is a complex literary work that explores themes such as class and social status, race, and the nature of good and evil.
