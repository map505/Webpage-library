About Fremantle Library

Fremantle Library is a place for everyone. Located within Walyalup Civic Centre, in the heart of Fremantle, it offers innovative services, programs and collections. A place to discover your next favourite read, to inspire your work, to think creatively, to learn through play, to connect, and to simply enjoy.



Monthly Newsletter

We have a monthly newsletter! It's one of the best places to get updates and fun information on what is happening in and around the library. Sign up now to get our end-of-year newsletter that will be a great retrospective on the last 12 months.


Gaming and Tech

Fremantle Library offers public access to gaming consoles and gaming networks, a 3D printer, and interactive screens. Featured is a dedicated youth zone with an Xbox Series S, a PlayStation 5, and Nintendo Switch available for free play. There is also a 3D printer service available.

In the children's library there is an interactive touch screen featuring Busy Things, a free educational eResource for 3–11-year-olds.


Current Services

  • Public computers - please note computers will automatically shut down 15 minutes before closing time
  • Printing - visit the PrinterOn site to send to our printer before you visit
  • Free 1-on-1 Tech Help - volunteer run, Tuesdays from 10am - 12pm and Wednesdays from 9:30am - 12:30pm
  • Public Wi-Fi
  • Access to power and data for personal charging and device usage
  • Scanning and photocopying
  • Justice of the Peace (volunteer run, 10am-1pm weekdays when volunteer available)
  • Community support worker under the LibraryConnect program - available Tuesday 1pm - 6pm, Wednesday 9am - 5pm, Thursday 1pm - 6pm and Saturday 9am - 2pm
  • Returns chute, open 24/7, located on Newman Court side across from the FOMO building
