The Great Gatsby


Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald


Publication Date: 1925


Genre: Tragedy, Fiction


Description: "The Great Gatsby" is a novel by American writer F. Scott Fitzgerald. Set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, the novel depicts narrator Nick Carraway's interactions with mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and Gatsby's obsession to reunite with his former lover, Daisy Buchanan. A quintessential novel of the American Dream, it explores themes of decadence, idealism, resistance to change, social upheaval, and excess, creating a portrait of the Roaring Twenties that has been described as a cautionary tale regarding the American Dream.
